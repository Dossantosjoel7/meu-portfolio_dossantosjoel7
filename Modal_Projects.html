AI Education Agents

01 Project Overview

A suite of autonomous multi-agent systems designed to revolutionize personalized learning. Leveraging large language models to act as specialized tutors, researchers, and assessment coordinators, creating a seamless end-to-end educational journey for students in computer science.

The Challenge

Standard AI chatbots lack pedagogical context and memory, leading to disjointed learning experiences and factual hallucinations in technical topics.

The Solution

An orchestrated agent architecture using RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to ground tutoring sessions in verified academic datasets.
